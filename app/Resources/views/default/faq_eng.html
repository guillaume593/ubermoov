General questions

Is account registration required?

Registering an account on UBERMOOV ' is only required if you want to access our services. This guarantees you easy and fast access to our services via our application.

Edit account settings ?

To change your name, e-mail address, phone number, or password: 1. In the main menu of the application, select "Settings". 2. Tap the bar displaying your profile information. 3. Tap the information you want to edit. 4. Make your changes and click Save. If you change your phone number, you will receive a verification code via SMS. Enter this code in the application to confirm the change. If you change your password, you will need to enter your current password. The password must contain at least 5 characters.

What is the currency used for all transactions?

You can get an estimate of the price of the delivery before ordering. An estimate of the price of the delivery is given as an indication and is in no way a guarantee, because the actual price may vary according to different sectors (traffic, weather, type of package). The estimate does not include discounts or promotions. Delivery price estimates are calculated based on routes and the volume of your packages that may not match those used during delivery.

How to order a delivery?

Via our application: UBERMOOV '                       If you see at the bottom of the application a cursor allowing you to select the type and number of packages:                       1. Use the slider to select the type of package.                       2. Press the black "COMMANDER" bar at the bottom of the screen.                       6. If a rate increase is currently in effect, your application will let you know before ordering the race.                       7. Wait for a delivery person to accept your order.                       8. Once your order is accepted, you will be able to see the position of your deliveryman on the map, as well as an estimated arrival time. You will receive an alert about 1 minute before arrival.

I want to sell my items - what are the steps?

  • Activate your account
  • Go to the Themes section and upload your theme
  • The next step is the approval step, which usually takes about 72 hours.

How much do I get from each sale?

When do I get paid?

Our standard payment plan provides for monthly payments. At the end of each month, all accumulated funds are transfered to your account. The minimum amount of your balance should be at least 70 USD.
