Is account registration required?

Registering an account on UBERMOOV ' is only required if you want to access our services. This guarantees you easy and fast access to our services via our application.

Comment commander une livraison ?

Via our application: UBERMOOV ' If you see at the bottom of the application a cursor allowing you to select the type and number of packages: 1. Use the slider to select the type of package. 2. In order to define your place of support, indicate the desired location by positioning your marker on the map, then enter an exact address in the location bar or enter a part of the address and select the exact address that appears in the search results. 3. Press the black bar DEFINE THE PLACE OF SUPPORT. The app will then display an estimated time of arrival for the nearby driver who has accepted your order. 4. Tap the destination bar, and then enter an address or choose one of the search results. You can add or change a destination at any time, before or after the order. 5. Press the black "COMMANDER" bar at the bottom of the screen. 6. If a rate increase is currently in effect, your application will let you know before ordering the race. 7. Wait for a delivery person to accept your order. 8. Once your order is accepted, you will be able to see the position of your deliveryman on the map, as well as an estimated arrival time. You will receive an alert about 1 minute before arrival.
